Peer Programming Tutorial

What is pair programming?


Pair programming is a collaborative process where two people work together to solve problems through code.

Why is pair programming important to DBC?


Pair programming is important to DBC for several reasons:

  • Pair progamming is becoming an industry standard with more and more companies adopting this practice in their development process.
  • It's beneficial from a productivity and social standpoint
  • Pair programming can help avoid hours of frustration by having an additional set of eyes to check your work.

What roles would pair programming involve?

Pair programming involves two distince roles. The navigator and the driver. The navigator defines the problem and develops the solution. The driver ensures the navigators thoughts are properly reflected on the screen.
